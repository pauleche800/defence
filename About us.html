BRIEF HISTORY OF OUR COLLEGE:

Holy Ghost College, Owerri was founded in January 1949, by the Catholic Archdiocese of Owerri. the College took its First external examinations with the Cambridge Oversea school Certificate, in 1953 and was the then centre for both government college and Emmanuel college for external examinations. in 1970 when the college was taken over by the former East Central State Government. it ws renamed Arugo High School. However, in recognition of the Catholic church as its founder and in respect of its old boys, it got back its name Holy Ghost College. in september 2004 the School with some other secondary School were de-aticulated into Junior and Secondary School and was later handed back to the Roman Catholic church in 2011 by His Excellency Dr.Ikedi Ohakim, former Governor of Imo State.
